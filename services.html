Wedding Receptions

No wedding is complete without a reception to mingle with family and friends! Make arrangements to host your reception in one of two designated gathering areas on the ground floor of the hotel.

  • Setup -
  • Ask to add reception setup to your stay for a small fee and you'll have access to our reception furnishings, decorations, and serving supplies! Our extra hands will make light work of your reception planning, leaving you time to focus on what's most important.
  • Catering -
  • Food services are also available thanks to arrangements with local businesses and restaurants!
  • Note: in order to provide guaranteed food service, please make arrangements with the hotel branch no later than 3 months ahead of the reception date.

For Full-Time Temple Missionaries

We want to help your mission go as smoothly as possible as you strive to bless God's children. We are offering temple missionaries in need of housing private kitchenette suites for the duration of their assignments (while vacancies persist).

Are you preparing to serve a full-time temple mission in one of our four locations? Contact us for more information about amenities and suite availability.
